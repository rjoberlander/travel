Your Coastal Journey

Three days of sun, surf, and California coastal luxury

9:50 AM

Coast Starlight Departure

Begin your journey aboard Amtrak's Coast Starlight, one of America's most scenic train routes. Enjoy panoramic views of the Pacific coastline as you travel from Los Angeles to Santa Barbara.

2.5 hour coastal journey
LA Union Station to SB
Book seats on the ocean side (right side of train heading north) for the most breathtaking coastal views!
12:00 PM

Waterfront Lunch

Arrive in Santa Barbara and enjoy a leisurely lunch at one of the waterfront restaurants. Savor fresh seafood while taking in views of the harbor and Channel Islands.

Harbor views
Fresh coastal seafood
Many restaurants offer happy hour 3-6 PM. Perfect timing for discounted appetizers and local wine pairings!
4:00 PM

Chase Palm Park

Stroll through this beautiful oceanfront park featuring art installations, a vintage carousel, and stunning beach access. Perfect for a relaxing afternoon walk along the coast.

Prime oceanfront location
Coastal art installations
7:00 PM

FisHouse Dinner

End your first day with dinner at this acclaimed seafood restaurant. Known for their fresh catches and innovative preparations, it's the perfect introduction to Santa Barbara's culinary scene.

Acclaimed coastal dining
Fresh local catch daily
Reservations recommended, especially for weekend evenings. Don't miss their signature coastal cioppino!
Hotel

Hotel Californian

Your home base for the weekend at this stunning Spanish Colonial Revival hotel. Located in the heart of the Funk Zone, you'll be steps away from galleries, tasting rooms, and restaurants.

Historic Funk Zone location
Luxury coastal accommodation
Historic property with modern coastal amenities. Rooftop bar offers stunning panoramic views of city and ocean!
9:30 AM

Santa Barbara Zoo

Start Sunday with a visit to this beautiful zoo overlooking the Pacific Ocean. Home to over 500 animals, it's perfectly sized for a morning visit with stunning coastal views throughout.

Breathtaking ocean views
2-3 hours of coastal wildlife
Don't miss the California sea lions and the charming miniature train ride with panoramic ocean vistas!
9:00 AM

MOXI Museum

Explore this innovative science and technology museum with interactive exhibits. MOXI offers hands-on experiences perfect for curious minds of all ages, from robotics to renewable energy displays.

Interactive coastal science
3-4 hours of exploration
Check their website for special oceanographic exhibitions and marine science workshops during your visit!
1:45 PM

Coast Starlight Return

Board the southbound Coast Starlight for your scenic return journey to Los Angeles. Enjoy your final coastal views and reflect on an amazing weekend adventure.

2.5 hour coastal farewell
SB to LA Union Station
Perfect timing for a final coastal meal on the train and capturing magical sunset photos over the Pacific!

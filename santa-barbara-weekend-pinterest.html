Coast Starlight Adventure

Begin your journey aboard Amtrak's legendary Coast Starlight, departing from Los Angeles Union Station. This spectacular rail journey hugs the Pacific coastline, offering views you simply can't see from the highway.

★★★★★ Scenic Train Journey • 3.5 Hours to Santa Barbara
TRAIN EXPERIENCE HIGHLIGHTS
🚂 Sightseer Lounge - Floor-to-ceiling windows
🌊 Pacific Ocean views - Left side seating
🥪 Café Car - Fresh snacks and beverages
📱 Free WiFi - Stay connected during journey
Pro tip: Sit on the left side of the train for the best ocean views as you cruise past beaches that are inaccessible by car!

Chase Palm Park & Boardwalk

Stroll along Santa Barbara's stunning 10-acre waterfront park expanded in 1998. The star attraction is the famous Shipwreck Playground modeled after the Winfield Scott paddle-wheeler that sank in 1853.

★★★★★ 4.5/5 • 32 Reviews • "Child Approved!"
PARK HIGHLIGHTS FROM VISITORS
🚢 Shipwreck Playground - Two structures (ages 2-5 & 5-12)
🐋 Surfacing Whales & Conch Shell Lighthouse sculptures
⛲ Fountains, lagoon & creek features
🎵 Summer Concerts in the Park venue
The ship's sinking bow forms a slide! Kids can scamper on bridges and netting in soft beach sand.

9:30 AM

Santa Barbara Zoo

Experience "the zoo with an ocean view" - 30 acres overlooking the Pacific, home to over 500 animals representing 146 species. The manageable size allows leisurely exploration in 2-3 hours.

★★★★☆ 4.5/5 • Ranked #7 of 128 Santa Barbara Attractions
CAN'T-MISS EXPERIENCES
🦒 Giraffe Feeding ($8) - "Eye-to-eye with Masai giraffes!"
🦘 Australian Walkabout - Walk freely among kangaroos
🐧 Humboldt Penguins - Above & underwater viewing
🦅 California Condors - Rare in captivity!
Pro tip: Check giraffe feeding times at the deck - arrive early! Free sensory backpacks available for sensitive children.

MOXI Museum

Ignite learning at this 17,000-square-foot interactive science wonderland opened in 2017. Located in the Funk Zone, MOXI offers three floors of hands-on STEAM exhibits for curious minds of all ages.

★★★★★ 213 Reviews • "2-3 hours of fun" • USA Today Top 10!
MUST-SEE EXHIBITS
🎸 Giant Guitar - 24-ft with crawl-through hole!
🌀 Wind Column Workshop - Test flight creations
🏎️ Speed Track - Build & race custom cars
🌅 Sky Garden - Rooftop with water sculpture
Pro tip: The rooftop water play sculpture is the kids' favorite - bring a change of clothes!

Chase Palm Park

Discover the crown jewel of Santa Barbara's beachfront parks! This 6-acre oceanside paradise features the iconic Shipwreck Playground, whale sculptures, and wide sandy beaches perfect for families.

★★★★☆ 4.4/5 • "Amazing playground!" • Free admission
PARK HIGHLIGHTS
🚢 Shipwreck Playground - Massive pirate ship structure!
🐋 Whale Sculptures - Climb on life-size whale statues
🏐 Beach Volleyball - Courts right on the sand
🎠 Santa Barbara Carousel - Historic restored carousel
Pro tip: Free parking on weekends! The playground is massive - kids could spend hours here. Bathrooms and snack bars available.

Santa Barbara FisHouse

Indulge in Santa Barbara's premier seafood destination! Located in the historic Funk Zone, FisHouse serves the freshest local catch with stunning harbor views and innovative Pacific Coast cuisine.

★★★★★ 4.6/5 • 950+ Yelp Reviews • "Best seafood in SB!"
SIGNATURE DISHES
🦞 Lobster Mac & Cheese - "Life-changing comfort food!"
🐟 Daily Fish Special - Caught fresh by local boats
🦀 Dungeness Crab Cakes - Award-winning recipe
🍷 Wine Pairings - Extensive Santa Barbara County selection
Pro tip: Reservations essential! Request harbor-view patio seating for sunset dining. Happy hour 4-6pm with discounted appetizers.

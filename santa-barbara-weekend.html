SATURDAY

9:50 AM - Departure

Coast Starlight Adventure

Begin your journey aboard Amtrak's legendary Coast Starlight, departing from Los Angeles Union Station. This spectacular rail journey hugs the Pacific coastline, offering views you simply can't see from the highway. Settle into the double-decker Superliner and make your way to the Sightseer Lounge car with floor-to-ceiling windows.

Pro tip: Sit on the left side of the train for the best ocean views as you cruise past beaches that are inaccessible by car!

12:00 PM - Arrival & Lunch

Waterfront Dining

Arrive in Santa Barbara and head straight to the waterfront for lunch. Choose from Chad's Cafe for all-day breakfast with ocean views, The Lark in the artsy Funk Zone for farm-to-table delights, or Brophy Bros. for the freshest catch of the day. Each offers a quintessential Santa Barbara dining experience.

The Funk Zone is right next to the train station - perfect for exploring local art galleries and wine tasting rooms!

Coast Starlight Return

Board the southbound Coast Starlight for your scenic journey home.
